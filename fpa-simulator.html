FP&A Simulator

Build Studio · FP&A · Commercial Finance · Simulation · Teaching · Scenario Design · AI · Python

/assets/img/fpa-simulator-visual.jpg

An interactive finance and commercial decision simulator for teaching FP&A, pricing, working capital, trade-offs, and decision-making under operational constraint.

What it does

The FP&A Simulator puts learners inside business scenarios where pricing, volume, supplier terms, customer behavior, inventory, cash flow, and financial statements interact. The goal is to teach judgment: how a decision moves through the business and what consequences follow.

No multiple-choice fantasy. Every decision has consequences.

Domains

FP&A Commercial Finance Simulation Teaching Scenario Design Decision Systems AI Python

Context

The FP&A Simulator grew out of teaching at CEFAM, INSEEC, UCLY / USAC, and emlyon business school — where the gap between textbook finance and operational reality is obvious within the first classroom session. Most finance courses teach the formulas. This project teaches the decisions.

The Build Studio pillar is the right home for this: it is practical, tool-focused, and connected to both teaching and commercial systems expertise.
