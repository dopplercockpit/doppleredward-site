System integrators configure software. I validate that commercial contracts, system logic, and financial outcomes are economically aligned — and I stay until they are.

My focus is the gap between commercial intent and financial outcome: where trade agreements stop surviving the enterprise, where platform logic diverges from commercial reality, and where deployment complexity creates the kind of margin leakage that doesn't show up until the quarter closes.

I am SAP-integrated — I validate commercial logic, governance, and data flows that ERP-centric stacks depend on. I don't sell generic implementation. I make commercial execution tell the truth in SD, FI, and COPA.

LOG_001

MARGIN RECOVERY & TRADE GOVERNANCE REBUILD

Client: Global Beauty & CPG Enterprise (~$394M business unit)
Context: Finance Director — P&L governance, trade finance transformation

Engagement:

  • Rebuilt end-to-end trade governance across a severely misaligned deployment
  • Automated claims and promotional finance workflows; eliminated manual settlement process
  • Hardened ERP alignment — SD/FI/COPA posting logic, accrual timing, Gross-to-Net integrity
  • Unified Finance and Sales reporting so both functions saw the same P&L reality

Outcome:

Recovered 330+ basis points of gross margin over three years. Streamlined GTM decision-making around promo, pricing, and trade funding.
