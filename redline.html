WHAT REDLINE IS

What is Redline?

Redline is a simulation engine that teaches financial decision-making, not spreadsheet key-mashing.

Set inside a fictional industrial company, Redline drops players into a live CFO office: inventory, capacity, working capital, customer contracts, and a board breathing down their necks. No multiple-choice fantasiesevery move flows through full financial statements and a real operating model.

Participants don't "learn concepts." They run a business under pressure:

  • Pricing, discounting, and trade terms
  • Volume risk, supply chain shocks, and production constraints
  • Cash flow crunches, credit risk, and audit scrutiny

Redline is built for the people who actually have to answer, "Why did we miss?"and for the educators and leaders who want them to miss less.

CORE SYSTEMS

Three integrated systems, one objective: teach the real cost of every decision.

MODULE_01 LIVE PROTOTYPE

DECISION ENGINE

The in-game company has a full financial backbone:

  • Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow
  • Inventory and production capacity
  • Customer and contract portfolio

Every decision (pricing, promo, volume commitments, supplier terms) drives real financial consequencesgood, bad, or ugly. No abstract "score," just the numbers you'd have to explain in a board pack.

MODULE_02 LIVE PROTOTYPE

SCENARIO & TELEMETRY LAYER

Redline runs as a sequence of scenariossupply shocks, demand spikes, cost increases, contract renegotiations.

Under the hood, a telemetry engine tracks:

  • Choices made (what, when, and in what order)
  • Impact on margin, liquidity, and risk
  • Time-to-decision and information used

This creates a decision fingerprint for each participant or team, making it easy to debrief: not just what they decided, but how they thought.

MODULE_03 EXPERIMENTAL

LLM-ASSISTED DYNAMICS

Redline uses a controlled GenAI layer to add semi-dynamic content:

  • News feeds that surface shocks and opportunities
  • Supplier and customer "emails" and draft contracts
  • Nudges and challenge prompts that stress-test the player's reasoning

The AI does not run the show; it adds noise and realism. The financial logic and scoring remain fully transparent and auditable.

WHERE REDLINE FITS

Built for serious training environments where role-play isn't enough and Excel isn't engaging.

UNIVERSITIES & BUSINESS SCHOOLS

  • Courses in Finance, FP&A, Strategy, Accounting, Operations, and RGM
  • Replace static case studies with live, multi-period simulations
  • Give students something more realistic than a clean Harvard case and a retrofitted answer key

CORPORATE FP&A, RGM & FINANCE ACADEMIES

  • Onboard new analysts and managers with a safe sandbox
  • Run team-based war games ahead of budget season or major investments

PRIVATE EQUITY & INVESTORS

  • Use Redline as a tabletop for management teams: how do they react under real constraints?
  • Stress-test pricing, capacity, and capital allocation instincts before handing over real money
