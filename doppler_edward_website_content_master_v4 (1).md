# Doppler/Edward Website Content Master v4 — Joshua Dopkowski

**Purpose:** Source content and structure for Joshua Dopkowski’s personal portfolio / website CV.

**Strategic reset:** The site should read like a clear professional menu, not an advertisement. The homepage is the main event: an interactive CV-style landing page that introduces Joshua and organizes his work into four tangible pillars.

**Audience:** Potential clients, Recruiters, hiring managers, former colleagues, project sponsors, PE / investor contacts, CPG / FMCG leaders, software vendors, consulting partners, education contacts, and people who have already had some initial contact with Joshua.

**Tone standard:** Clear, factual, warm, direct, useful. No chest-beating. No abstract self-mythology. No generic consulting fog. No negative constructions built around “I am not…” or “X is not…” or "we're not just X... we're Y" unless absolutely necessary.

# 13. Content Guardrails

## Avoid

- “I am not…” framing
- “X is not about Y…” framing unless rewritten positively
- Pretentious abstractions
- Unsupported declarations
- Overclaiming
- Arrogant tone
- Generic consulting phrases
- Excessive buzzwords
- Too much negativity in headlines
- Language that makes Joshua sound like a firm instead of a person

## Prefer

- Concrete nouns
- Direct verbs
- Evidence-based claims
- Menu-style descriptions
- Clear use cases
- Practical examples
- Specific credentials and work history
- Plain explanations of value
- Confident but grounded language

## Examples of Preferred Rewrites

### Instead of:

“I am not a narrow specialist.”

### Use:

“My work connects multiple functions: commercial, finance, IT, operations, vendors, and leadership.”

### Instead of:

“RGM is not a dashboard.”

### Use:

“RGM works when planning, execution, settlement, and measurement stay connected.”

### Instead of:

“AI is not strategy.”

### Use:

“AI is most useful when it improves reasoning, visibility, and decision quality.”

It should not try to prove everything at once. It should introduce Joshua clearly, organize the work into four understandable pillars, and let people drill into the area that matters to them.

The homepage is the main event. The pillar pages are the deeper menu. Projects show proof of build capacity. The blog shows thinking over time. The ANSI Art Gallery preserves personality and craft.
