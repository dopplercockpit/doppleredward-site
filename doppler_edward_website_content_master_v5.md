# 4. Pillar Page — Systems & Execution

## Page Title

Systems & Execution

## Page Subtitle

CPG ERP · RGM / TPM / TPO · Functional Design · Deployment · Stabilization

## Mode Label

*Functional · Tactical — the architecture and the mechanic*

## Intro Copy

Commercial software only works when the business logic it encodes is actually correct.

This pillar covers the functional and tactical layer: configuring commercial systems, validating that the design reflects what the business intended, aligning ERP behavior with commercial agreements, and resolving the failures that appear before, during, and after go-live.

The work is hands-on. It requires knowing how promotional mechanics, pricing conditions, accrual logic, settlement paths, and ERP posting behavior interact — and being able to diagnose where they break and why.

---

## Promotion Configuration & Commercial Logic

Confirm the system accurately reflects commercial behavior by configuring and testing:

- Customer and product hierarchies
- Pricing conditions mapping and reporting
- Pricing waterfall integrity
- Off-invoice promotional allowances setup
- Lump-sum vs variable funding configuration
- Customer-specific promotion mechanics
- Three-way match mapping with ERP
- Contract clause to configuration rule alignment

---

## Settlement Action Mapping

Ensure settlement actions are correctly configured and functioning across promotion types and commercial scenarios:

- Billback / chargeback / rebate configuration
- Timing issues affecting budget (e.g. early payment discount windows)
- Debit / credit memo generation and posting
- Missing settlement action identification
- Commercial intent and system logic alignment
- Settlement path diagnosis
- Promotion-to-settlement rule alignment
- Exception scenario mapping

---

## Accrual Logic Testing

Test accrual generation across key promotion scenarios and resolve issues as discovered:

- Planned vs actual accrual behavior analysis
- Timing mismatch analysis
- Accrual trigger testing
- Over / under-accrual root-cause analysis
- Accrual releases and reversals
- Trade term governance mapping

---

## Contract-to-System Mapping

Translate business terms into system objects so that commercial intent is represented in the tool:

- Allowance structure mapping
- Pricing term interpretation into TPM logic
- Funding rule documentation
- Contract clause to configuration rule alignment

---

## Delivered Baseline and Template Quality Assurance

Review, interpret, and accurately assess delivered templates, mechanics, and preconfigured baselines:

- Promotion template accuracy
- Planning baseline validity
- Market template fit/gap assessment
- Reusable object integrity

---

## UAT Case Design

Build focused test scenarios around the commercial mechanics that most often fail in real deployments:

- Promo lifecycle test scripts
- Settlement scenarios
- Pre-flight checklist with required testing results for cutover
- Edge-case stress testing

---

## Master Data Management

Resolve master data mapping issues affecting planning, execution, and reporting:

- Customer hierarchy
- Product master data and product hierarchy
- Sales data mash-ups and mapping
- Delivered baselines and aggregation / disaggregation
- Funding objects

---

## ERP Integration — Posting Logic and Condition Alignment

### Promotion Posting Alignment

Ensure TPM / TPx-generated postings align correctly to ERP financial objects and expected accounting treatment:

- GL mapping accuracy
- Posting flow and reporting
- Condition mapping
- Error logs and error diagnosis

### SD Pricing and Condition Alignment

Ensure commercial conditions in SAP / ERP align with TPM logic and real contract behavior:

- Pricing conditions vs posted allowances
- Condition record logic vs TPM logic
- Duplicate condition identification
- Pricing / allowances flow: prioritization, stacking, and pricing controls

### Contract-to-ERP Scenario Tracing

Trace commercial scenarios from trade terms through TPM mechanics to ERP postings:

- Promotion to invoice
- Claims / deductions
- Accrual to settlement
- End-to-end promotional allowances scenario

### Integration Issue Triage

Investigate where data or logic breaks between TPM / RGM platform and ERP:

- Build custom tools to investigate inbound / outbound messages (JSON / XML / CSV)
- Identify incorrect fields
- Data Lake mapping mismatches
- Middleware handshake failures

## Business-to-Technical Defect Translation

Turn user complaints into usable technical issue statements for vendor, support, or IT teams:

- "Numbers are wrong" issues converted into reproducible defects
- Root-cause hypothesis framing
- Vendor escalation support
- Distinguishing system bugs, data issues, design gaps, configuration choices, change requests, and adoption failures

SalonCentric was L'Oréal PPD's $1.1B national salon distribution operation. Direct P&L responsibility: $394M — a mixed portfolio of PPD brands and partner / competitive brands including Dyson, GHD, Bio Ionic, Revlon, Coty, and Olaplex.

The commercial model was structurally complex. Promotional funding moved simultaneously across AP debits against manufacturers, AR credits in L'Oréal brand local entities, and downstream debit / credit memo settlement with salon professional customers — across four channel layers (sub-distribution, company-owned stores, street sales, e-commerce) and six distribution centers across the US. Operations spanned Tampa Bay, Hudson Yards (New York), and New Jersey simultaneously.

**What was built:**  
The trade governance and promotions finance function was built from scratch: business case authored, executive sponsorship secured, seven-person team hired and led, function deployed from zero to a governed, auditable operation.

**Scope of governance:**
- Promotional funding and trade event agreements (TEAs)
- Manufacturer distributor agreements
- Trade event mechanics, inventory management tiers, compliance chargeback programs
- EDI requirements and SAP 3-way match compliance
- Anti-diversion controls
- AP / AR settlement flows — simultaneous, bidirectional, multi-entity
- Early payment discount windows
- Gross-to-Net integrity
- Period-end accrual routines, deduction management, claims validation

**Result:**  
330+ basis points of gross margin recovery over three years through disciplined claims automation, ERP alignment hardening, promotional finance workflow redesign, and Gross-to-Net governance.
