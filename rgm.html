THE FULL-STACK RGM CYCLE

01

Strategy & Planning (TPM)

Moving from "roll forward last year's plan" to zero-based, invest-for-growth planning. Linking promo calendars to P&L, brand objectives, and retailer strategy — not just to volume gaps.

02

Execution & Settlement

Industrializing the "unsexy" work — claims, deductions, settlement — so Sales can sell instead of reconciling spreadsheets. Reducing leakage, write-offs, and disputes that silently erode margin.

03

Trade Governance Architecture (Enforcement Layer)

Strategy without enforcement becomes leakage. We build the trade operating system, enforced by contract when needed: channel rules, funding logic, inventory economics, and compliance consequences -- so RGM survives contact with reality.

  • Trade spend governance (funding rules, accrual discipline, dispute windows)
  • Balance-sheet aware trade (inventory risk allocation and lifecycle rules)
  • Channel integrity (anti-diversion; channel conflict management)
  • Monetized non-compliance (chargebacks and enforceable consequences)

RGM only works when all three loops are wired together. That's the operating model we design, fix, and govern.
